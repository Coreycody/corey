About Me
I'm Corey, a 14-year-old who loves sports, music, and a good challenge. Soccer is my jam—I thrive on the field, not just for the game itself but for the teamwork and fun it comes with. Over the years, I've learned that working together effectively can lead to amazing outcomes, both in sports and in life. Academically, I've had some cool achievements too. I've won my school's spelling bee two years in a row, and on my second go-round, I snagged fourth place. It's been interesting diving into words and languages, figuring out the nuances and meanings behind them. Besides spelling, music piques my interest. I play flute, piccolo, and piano, and there's something magical about making music. When I'm not on the field or practicing music, you can usually find me immersed in video games. They're my go-to for unwinding and testing my strategic thinking skills. And occasionally, I do enjoy reading too, especially when a story grabs me and won't let go. Overall, I'm just a guy who loves staying active, challenging myself intellectually, and exploring different interests. Each day brings new opportunities to learn and grow, and I'm all in for the adventure.
